One of my most favorite software features ever is the [Windows Spotlight](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/configuration/windows-spotlight/?pivots=windows-11). The built-in Windows 10/11 feature that automatically rotates your lock screen and desktop backgrounds, along with with tags populated with geographic location, well curated trivia and facts about the place you're looking at. Clicking the tag opens a Bing search map of the spot. A great way to learn about a new place.
